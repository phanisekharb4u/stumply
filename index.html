📖 HOW TO USE STUMPLY
Follow these steps to run your cricket tournament
1
⚙️ Create Your Tournament
After logging in, click the ⚙️ Admin tab in the top navigation.
① Enter your Tournament Name (e.g. "Street Cricket Cup 2026")
② Enter Edition (e.g. "Season 1") and Year
③ Select Overs Format — T20, T10, 8-over, or Custom
④ Click 💾 Save Tournament
2
🏏 Add Teams & Players
Still in ⚙️ Admin, scroll down to Add Teams.
① Enter Team Name, Code (2-3 letters, e.g. MI), City
② Enter 11 player names — these are required (numbered in gold)
③ Optionally add up to 3 substitutes (numbered in amber)
④ Click ✅ Add Team
⑤ Repeat for every team in your tournament
3
📋 Schedule a Match
Click the 📋 Matches tab, then click + Schedule.
① Select Team A and Team B from your teams
② Choose Match Stage — League, Quarter Final, Semi Final, Final
③ Enter Venue, Date, and Umpire names
④ Playing XI is auto-filled — adjust if needed
⑤ Click 📅 Schedule
4
🔴 Score the Match Live
Click ▶ Start on the match card.
① Set the toss — who won and chose to bat or bowl
② Select 2 opening batsmen and the opening bowler
③ The 🔴 Live tab opens automatically
④ Tap the scoring buttons for each ball:
   • Dot, 1, 2, 3, 4, 6 — for runs
   • WIDE / NO BALL / BYE / LEG BYE — for extras
   • ⚡ WICKET — enter dismissal details
⑤ Score updates automatically after every ball — viewers see it live!
5
👁 Share Live Scores
Share the link so anyone can watch scores update in real time.
① In ⚙️ Admin → scroll to Share Link → click 📋 Copy Link
② Send the link to friends via WhatsApp / SMS
③ They open it → enter your username → scores update every second
④ No login needed for viewers — read-only access
⑤ After the match ends → 🏅 Select MOTM → view full stats in 🏆 History
